How to use KittyPaint!

On mobile devices:
  • Touch and drag to draw
  • Drag with 2 fingers to move
On desktop:
  • Left click to draw
  • Right click and drag or middle click and drag to move
  • OR hold m and move mouse to move
  • OR use arrow keys
Click the "+" button for advanced options.
